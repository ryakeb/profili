Welcome to Profili, where excellence in textiles meets over 30 years of industry experience. We are a premier provider of high-quality fabrics sourced exclusively from the finest Italian manufacturers.

About Us

With decades of expertise, our commitment is to deliver nothing short of the best, ensuring that every piece of textile we offer upholds the highest standards of quality, luxury, and durability.

Our journey in the textile industry began three decades ago, fueled by a passion for exquisite fabrics and a dedication to craftsmanship. Over the years, we've forged strong partnerships with renowned Italian mills, allowing us to bring you a curated selection of textiles that embody the elegance and sophistication Italy is known for.

At Profili, we understand the importance of choosing the right fabrics for your projects, whether it's for fashion, interior design, or bespoke tailoring. That's why we offer personalized services tailored to meet your specific needs.
Our team of experts is here to guide you in selecting the perfect materials that not only meet but exceed your expectations.

Experience the difference that 30 years of dedication to quality makes. With Profili, you are assured of products that stand the test of time, just like our legacy in the textile industry.

Our Services

Premium Textile Supply

We provide an extensive range of luxury textiles, sourced directly from Italy’s most prestigious mills. Whether you’re looking for elegant silks, rich velvets, fine linens, or bespoke fabrics, we offer the finest materials to meet your specific requirements.

Custom Fabric Sourcing

Our deep connections with Italian textile manufacturers allow us to offer a custom fabric sourcing service. If you have specific fabric needs or are looking for something unique, we can source or even commission exclusive textiles that are perfectly suited to your project.

Textile Consulting Leverage

Our decades of experience with our textile consulting service. Whether you’re a designer, manufacturer, or retailer, our team can provide expert advice on fabric selection, trends, and applications to help you make informed decisions.

Bespoke Textile Design

For clients seeking something truly unique, we offer bespoke textile design services. Collaborate with us to create custom patterns, textures, and colorways that bring your creative vision to life.

Wholesale & Bulk Orders

We cater to both small and large-scale projects with our wholesale and bulk order services. No matter the size of your order, we ensure timely delivery and consistent quality across all our products.

Sustainable Textiles

As part of our commitment to sustainability, we offer a selection of eco-friendly textiles made from organic or recycled materials. Our sustainable fabrics maintain the luxury and quality we’re known for while supporting environmentally responsible practices.
